## Surface Movement Control (Ground)

### Flight Plan Compliance

Ensure **all flight plans** are checked for compliance with the approved WF Route the `OHCH2`:

`ABTOL Y266 GURBO Y804 ATBUB H159 MESIX`

If an aircraft has filed an _incorrect_ route and you need to give an amended clearance.

### World Flight Teams

[WorldFlight](https://www.worldflight.center/official-teams) Teams shall have `STS/STATE` added to their remarks, and `WF TEAM` added to their Global Ops Field, to ensure they receive priority.

### Runway Selection

Runway 23L operations are preferred as it provides improved airspace utilization for ATMA. These operations can occur with up to a 10 kt tailwind.

### DCL

DCL is not available at NZOH, all clearances shall be by voice.

### Arrivals

All medium jets can expect to park at the 3 Squadron Apron or 5 Squadron Apron.

All heavy jets can expect to park at the Northern Apron.

### Departures

All departures shall use holding point `C` or `A1`. Aircraft shall be told to monitor tower prior to reaching the holding point.

Pushback/Startup shall be metered such that there are no more than five aircraft at the holding point.

## Aerodrome Control (Tower)

### Runway Vacating Instructions

Aircraft must vacate the runway to the north. `A2` is not available due size restrictions.

All medium jets can expect to park at the 3 Squadron Apron or 5 Squadron Apron.
All heavy jets can expect to park at the Northern Apron.

### Departure Spacing

Ensure that a minimum of **90 seconds** spacing is applied between subsequent departures from the same runway.
