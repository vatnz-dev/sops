## Sector Responsibilities

### Auckland Radio (Tasman) (ART)

ART is responsible for ensuring the separation of aircraft in the enroute phase of flight.

For this event ART is operating as a surveillance sector with radar minimums (5 nm laterally) and VHF comms.

### Oceanic Radar (OCR)

OCR is responsible for initial sequencing, issuing STAR clearances, and issuing an initial descent for aircraft bound for NZAA.

### Raglan (RAN)

RAN is responsible for handling diversions to NZOH from NZAA.

### Taranaki (NAK)

NAK is responsible for initial sequencing, issuing STAR clearances, and issuing an initial descent for aircraft bound for NZOH.

It is also responsible for the diverting aircraft from NZAA.

### OCR to ATMA

Voiceless for all aircraft:

- Assigned `A110`; and
- Tracking via `ARADI`

### NAK to OTMA

Voiceless for all aircraft:

- Assigned `A110`; and
- Tracking via `BINIT`

### OHA to OTMA

Voiceless for all aircraft:

- Assigned `A110`; and
- Tracking via `IDLUR`
